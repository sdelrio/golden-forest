# 🚀 Introducing Qwen3.5 Small Model Series

> **More intelligence, less compute.**

A new series of small language models designed for efficiency without sacrificing capabilities.

## Model Overview

| Model    | Parameters | Best For                          |
|----------|------------|-----------------------------------|
| **Qwen3.5-0.8B**  | 0.8B       | Edge devices, ultra-fast inference |
| **Qwen3.5-2B**    | 2B         | Tiny, fast, great for edge device  |
| **Qwen3.5-4B**    | 4B         | Lightweight agents                 |
| **Qwen3.5-9B**    | 9B         | Gap-closer, compact powerhouse     |

## Key Features

All models share the same Qwen3.5 foundation with:

- ✅ **Native multimodal** capabilities
- ✅ **Improved architecture** for better efficiency
- ✅ **Scaled RL** training benefits
- ✅ **Base models** also released for research

## Model Positioning

### 0.8B & 2B Models
- Tiny footprint
- Ultra-fast inference
- Perfect for edge constraints
- Great for quick responses

### 4B Model
- Surprisingly strong for its size
- Excellent multimodal foundation
- Ideal for lightweight agents
- Good balance of speed and intelligence

### 9B Model
- Compact but impressive
- Closing gap with larger models
- Solid performance for resource-conscious deployments

## Why Small Models Matter

- **Lower compute requirements**
- **Faster inference times**
- **Better for edge deployment**
- **Lower cost of operation**
- **More practical for local use**
